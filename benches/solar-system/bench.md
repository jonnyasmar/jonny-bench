Build a highly interactive education tool to teach kids about our solar system.

Requirements:

- Pure client-side. The finished product must be a static site: an index.html at the repo root (or in dist/ if you use a build step) that works with no server-side code.
- You will be graded on how engaging and effective of a tool it is.
- The tool will be used by a 4 and 7 year old and they should be experts on the must-know information about our solar system by the time they're done.
- You may use any libraries you see fit, such as Three.js or you can build it in native tech like Canvas/WebGL.

You have one shot: when you stop, whatever is in the repo is the submission.
